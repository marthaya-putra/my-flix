## Goal
Confirm (or refute) the deficit-retry-loop hypothesis for issue #3 (tv re-loops its stage messages from the top) before fixing. Logs are temporary, clearly tagged, removed once confirmed.

## Why these points
The re-loop can only come from one of three sources. These log points disambiguate all three:
- **Deficit loop** → server emits `progress finding_titles` for tv a 2nd time in the *same* stream; client sees one `consumeStream`, one continuous event sequence.
- **Spurious new stream** → client `consumeStream` / mount effect fires again.
- **Premature groupEnd** → client `dispatch` sees `groupEnd` for tv before items land.
